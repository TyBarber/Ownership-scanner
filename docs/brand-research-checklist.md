## Evidence required for every proposed edge

- Official brand/company page, official acquisition announcement, SEC/government record, or reputable business publication
- Source title, HTTPS URL, publisher, source type, publication date when available, and retrieval date
- Explicit `verified`, `probable`, or `unresolved` status
- Confidence from 0 to 1 and last-verified date
